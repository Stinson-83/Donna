04 · Rules

What not to do.

No decorations
No dot, underline, box, or swash. The italic is the mark. Past explorations are archived, not shipped.
Never redraw
The mark is set in live type. Do not outline, do not redraw as a path. Use font-style: italic, font-weight: 500.
No signal colors
Moss, amber, oxblood are states, not brand colors. The mark appears only in ink, rust, or paper.
Minimum size 14 px
Below 14 px, use the "d" monogram instead of the full wordmark.
One rust per screen
If the wordmark appears in rust, nothing else on that screen may be rust.
