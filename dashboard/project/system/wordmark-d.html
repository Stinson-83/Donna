02 · D · at size

The recommended mark, bigger.

Italic 500 with rust underline at 0.04em, offset 0.12em. On dark and rust surfaces, the underline flips to paper to preserve the signature feel without introducing a new color.

Paper · primary donna the small things kept.
Surface · card donna a hand on your sleeve.
Ink · splash donna a quieter kind of help.
Rust · rare donna not loud. just there.
03 · D · scale ladder

From splash to masthead.

The underline thickness is relative (0.04em) so it scales with the mark. Below 20px, drop the underline — the serif italic alone is enough.
